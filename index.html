How It Works

The Process

From first contact to final delivery — every step crafted with intentionality and cinematic precision.

01

Discovery Call

We connect over WhatsApp or a call to understand your vision, event details and what emotions you want immortalised.

02

Proposal & Brief

I prepare a tailored creative brief and a transparent quote — no hidden charges. You approve every element before we begin.

03

Raw Footage Review

All your footage is shared, organised and reviewed. I select the most powerful moments — the tears, the laughter, the glances.

04

Cinematic Edit

Color grading, sound design, music sync and motion effects are applied — transforming footage into a narrative masterpiece.

05

Review & Revisions

You receive a private preview link. One or two rounds of revisions are included — your satisfaction is non-negotiable.

06

4K Final Delivery

Delivered in 4K via Google Drive. High-resolution, ready to treasure, share, and re-live for decades to come.
